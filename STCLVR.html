STCLVR
https://stclvr.bandcamp.com/
STCLVR (streetcleaver) is the brain-child of western NY based non-binary artist G. Mor.
Utilizing a small array of table-top electronics and reasonably outdated software, STCLVR composes nasty, brooding, and heavy hitting harsh industrial "pop"
tunes as well as murky, loopy power electronics pieces.
STCLVR has been destroying stages and basement floors along the east coast for nearly a decade with their cathartic, high energy performances.
