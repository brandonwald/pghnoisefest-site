DRAOIDH




https://orbtapes.bandcamp.com/album/calamity-of-man
DRAOIDH is a dark ambient duo from central Pennsylvania that has been active since 2019.
Their music is primarily influenced by the sounds and motifs of the occult and relies heavily on magnetic tape as an instrument and drone as a mode.
The pair's most recent release, "Calamity of Man" (Orb Tapes, 2024), explores themes including mind control, bilocation,
and ancient Gaelic mythology and has been described as "dystopian ritual music" (African Paper).
