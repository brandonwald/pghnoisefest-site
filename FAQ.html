Q: What is Pittsburgh Noise Fest?
A: Pittsburgh Noise Fest is a 2 day festival featuring harsh noise, power electronics, and experimental music.

Q: Can I play a set at Pittsburgh Noise Fest?
A: No, Sorry.  The lineup is invite-only

Q: Is the fest all-ages?
A: The fest is 18+ and we will be carding at the door. Underage drinking will not be tolerated.

Q: Is there re-entry?
A: Yes! You will receive a wristband for re-entry.

Q: Will ear protection be provided?
A: Yes, although we recommend bringing your own as well.

Q: Will tickets be available at the door?
A: Probably not.  If the fest does not sell out then yes but at an increased cost. Watch for announcements.

Q: Whats the best way to get to the fest by public transportation?
A: The 43, 48 and the 51 bus lines all travel along Warrington Ave.  Little Giant is itself just a block off of Warrington.

Q: Can I table merch at PNF?
A: At this time all spot for merch are reserved for the acts that are playing.
