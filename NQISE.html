Noise With A Q also known as Neurodivergent Queer Inclusive Sonic Euphoria or NQISE seeks to demystify music showing everyone and anyone can make noise.
NQISE uses found objects and amplification to create noise that is simply enjoyable to their ear and feels good (in autistic stimming type way).
Pieces typically focus on trans and mad liberation as well as climate devastation. NQISE is a reminder that “there are no rules” - Everywhere Everything All At Once
