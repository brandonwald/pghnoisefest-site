Jules Malice has been organizing creative endeavors and community programs for over 16 years.
They perform live as a VJ and DJ under the Malzof moniker.
They utilize digital media, paint, installation, performance, sound and audience interaction to create unique creative and enriching experiences.
Their artistic production focuses on building community, audio-visual experiences and speaking truth.
They utilize playfulness, maximalism, and imagined environments as critical elements in their work.
