Page 27




https://www.page27.com

Page 27 is a noise/experimental group that has been active in Colorado since 1994.

Page 27 was created in 1994 by John Gross and Rasmussen, and many others.
The group formed together in the mid 90s when they were working together to make independent zines and decided to put some of those ideas to sound.
Over the years the membership has changed with as many as 7 people in the group at one time.

Many great people have played in Page 27 such as Todd Novosad, Christian Havener, Zack Tourje, Eric Lonan, Ezra Nye, and many others have added much to the group and changed the sound.
