slowdanger, a multidisciplinary performance entity co-founded by anna thompson and taylor knight, create work at the intersection of movement, sound, and technology.
Their music fuses power electronics, noise, and musique concrète influences with processed vocals and physical performance; producing visceral environments for catharsis.
Their practice blurs boundaries between dance, performance art, and experimental sound.
In addition to their work as slowdanger, they contribute collaboratively to the ensemble Black Leather Jesus through physical performance and sound.
