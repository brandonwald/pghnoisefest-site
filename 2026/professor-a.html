PROFESSOR A




https://woodroomcollective.bandcamp.com/album/kill-your-colonizers-volume-4

PROFESSOR A (Bison Squad, Media Bias) is based in Philadelphia, PA.
His work deals with themes of anti-colonialism, afro-futurism, and his familial and cultural heritage.
He uses synthesizers, effects pedals, vocal samples, and lyrics evocative of confessional poetry to create music ranging from soft drones to harsh cacophony.
