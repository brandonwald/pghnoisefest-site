Brandon Wald




https://www.blackringrituals.com

Brandon Wald has been making Noise Music since 2011.
He was originally based in Fargo, ND where he ran a weekly two hour radio show dedicated to Noise Music called Black Ring Rituals from 2014 to 2023.
He is the Owner/Operator of Black Ring Rituals Records which he founded in 2015.  Previous and current projects include: Monowolf, Support Unit, Piss Enema, Incendiary Cult, amongst others.
He was featured in the award winning documentary "The Noise Of Fargo" and has been interviewed by Bandcamp Daily and Kvlt Nation.
He is known for releasing on a wide variety of physical media formats including Hit Clips, Recording Wire, ORB Drive Discs, Betamax, and many others.
In 2016 he founded the Fargo Noise Fest, which continues on to this day under different leadership (and with his full support and blessing).
In 2025 he co-founded the Pittsburgh Noise Fest with Cleaner Tapes.
