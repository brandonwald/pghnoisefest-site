Bacillus




https://bacillus.bandcamp.com/

Originally from Ohio, now based in Seattle, Bacillus has been spreading sonic pathogens since 1993, and has quickly been established as one of the influential Americanoise projects of the 90's.
Using mangled tapes, broken vinyl, battered electronics, dying equipment, and glue-cast records, Bacillus infects with a harsh dirty soundtrack of entropy and decay with a singular focus on disease and epidemics.
With the previous warnings of an impending pandemic having come true, the message of Bacillus is still just as vital and shouldn't be ignored.
This will be the second ever Bacillus outbreak in Pittsburgh.
